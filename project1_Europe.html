Ticket to Ride

A unified transportation system working across the entire continent. Europe was where the public transport was born, first buses and trains appeared in European countries and over the years a complex transportation network developed. Europe is a culturally diverse continent so something that unifies it is the historic heritage, namely the Medieval culture with its distinct visual style and intricate mythology. Of particular interest are some of the mythical creatures that look truly fantastical and sometimes bizarre. This is what became the key visual element of the project.
